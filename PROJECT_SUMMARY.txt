────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  WHAT IS EASYFACT?
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

EASYfact is a multi-tenant invoice management SaaS platform built for
Algerian/French-speaking companies. It allows companies to digitize their
invoice workflow — from photo upload and OCR extraction, to review,
approval, and reporting — all inside a single collaborative workspace.

Key differentiators:
  · OCR-powered invoice extraction (Tesseract.js, EN + FR)
  · Role-based access for Directors, Accountants, and Employees
  · Contract management with auto-expiry for Accountant members
  · Real-time team chat (channels + direct messages)
  · Subscription-gated access with per-plan invoice and user limits
  · Full audit trail on every invoice status change
  · AI assistant (Llama 3.3 70B via Groq) with workspace context


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  USER ROLES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  PERSONAL (Normal)
    Individual account with a personal workspace. No team features.
    Manages their own invoices independently.

  DIRECTOR
    Owner of a company workspace. Full administrative control.
    Manages team, subscription, and company profile.

  ACCOUNTANT
    External accountant contracted to a company.
    Has a contract_start and contract_end date.
    Can validate/approve invoices. Auto-removed when contract expires.

  EMPLOYEE
    Regular company staff member.
    Can upload and submit invoices for review.
    Can only see their own submissions.

  ADMIN
    System-level superuser. Not tied to any company.
    Manages all users, companies, and invoices globally.

  ╔══════════════════════════════════╗
  ║  PERSONAL USER                   ║
  ╚══════════════════════════════════╝

  · As a personal user, I can register and create my own personal workspace.
  · As a personal user, I can upload invoice images and have them processed
    by OCR to extract vendor, amount, date, and invoice number automatically.
  · As a personal user, I can review and correct extracted fields before saving.
  · As a personal user, I can view all my invoices with their status.
  · As a personal user, I can filter and search invoices by vendor,

  · As a personal user, I can upgrade my plan at any time through the
    billing page with a credit card.
  · As a personal user, I can update my profile (name, email, password).
  · As a personal user, I can use the AI assistant to ask questions
    about my invoices (totals, vendors, trends).

  · As a Director, I can upgrade or renew the subscription plan at any time,

  · As a Director, I can view all current workspace members with their

  · As a Director, I can change a member's role.
  · As a Director, I can accept or reject leave requests submitted by members.
  · As a Director, I can accept or reject contract renewal requests from

  · As a Director, I can see all invoices submitted in my workspace,

  · As a Director, I can view the full status history of any invoice.
  · As a Director, I can delete any invoice.
  · As a Director, I can upload and submit invoices.

  · As a Director, I can see recent activity in the workspace.
  · As a Director, I can see a preview of the most recently submitted invoices.

  · As a Director, I retain access to Settings and Subscription pages even

  Communication
  · As a Director, I can use the team chat to message employees and
    accountants in workspace channels or via direct messages.
  · As a Director, I see an unread message badge on the Chat link

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  FEATURES & MODULES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  AUTHENTICATION (auth)
  ─────────────────────
  · Email/password registration with 6-digit email verification
  · JWT-based authentication (24-hour tokens)
  · Forgot password / reset password via email code
  · Multi-workspace support: switch between personal and company workspaces
  · Passwords hashed with bcryptjs (10 rounds)

  OCR — INVOICE EXTRACTION (ocr / ai)
  ─────────────────────────────────────
  · Accepts JPEG, PNG, PDF uploads
  · Preprocesses images: grayscale, sharpen, normalize, gamma correction
  · Runs Tesseract.js with English and French language models
  · Extracts: vendor name, invoice number, total amount, invoice date, due date
  · Normalizes date formats: DD/MM/YYYY, DD.MM.YYYY, written month names
  · Normalizes amounts: strips currency symbols (TND, $, €, £, DA)
  · Saves extracted fields to extracted_fields table per invoice

  INVOICE WORKFLOW (invoices)
  ────────────────────────────
  Statuses: draft → pending_review → approved / rejected → paid / archived

  · Employees submit invoices → status becomes pending_review
  · Accountants/Directors review and approve or reject with comment
  · Every status change is logged to status_history with who, when, comment
  · Notifications sent to all relevant parties on each status change
  · Role-based visibility: employees see only their own, accountants/directors
    see all

  DOCUMENTS (documents)
  ──────────────────────
  · Multiple file attachments per invoice
  · Files stored in server/uploads/ with timestamp-prefixed names
  · Primary document flag for the main invoice scan
  · Role-based deletion permissions

  TEAM MANAGEMENT (invitations + workspace)
  ──────────────────────────────────────────
  · Companies have unique 8-character join codes
  · Join requests (employee/accountant) require director approval
  · Accountants get contract_start and contract_end dates on acceptance
  · Leave requests require director approval
  · Renewal requests: accountants may submit within 30 days of expiry
  · Cron job runs daily to auto-remove accountants with expired contracts
  · Member role updates and removal by Director

  NOTIFICATIONS (notifications)
  ──────────────────────────────
  · In-app notification bell with unread count badge
  · Polled every 30 seconds in the background
  · Notification types: info, success, warning, error
  · Mark individual or all notifications as read
  · Action URLs link directly to the relevant resource
  · Triggered by: invoice events, membership events, subscription expiry

  REAL-TIME CHAT (chat)
  ──────────────────────
  · Workspace channels (e.g. #general, custom channels)
  · Direct messages between any two workspace members
  · #general channel auto-created for every workspace on first visit
  · Real-time message delivery via Socket.io (JWT authenticated)
  · Typing indicators ("Alice is typing…")
  · Unread message count badge on sidebar Chat link (polled every 15s)
  · Message history loaded on conversation open (50 messages, paginated)
  · Messages grouped by date with separators (Today, Yesterday, full date)
  · Bubble UI: own messages right-aligned (primary color), others left-aligned
  · New Channel modal and New DM modal with member search
  · Per-conversation unread count badges

  REPORTS & ANALYTICS (invoices/reports)
  ────────────────────────────────────────
  · Monthly invoice count and amount trend charts
  · Status distribution (approved vs rejected vs pending)
  · Top 5 vendors by invoice count
  · Employee leaderboard (submissions per user)
  · Selectable periods: 30 days, 90 days, 180 days, 1 year
  · Export formats: CSV, PDF (jsPDF), DOCX, full ZIP package
  · Dark-mode compatible export dropdowns

  SUBSCRIPTIONS (subscription)
  ─────────────────────────────
  Company plans:
    · Starter     — $49/mo  — 200 invoices, 10 users
    · Business    — $149/mo — 1,000 invoices, 50 users
    · Professional— $349/mo — 5,000 invoices, 200 users
    · Enterprise  — $999/mo — Unlimited invoices and users

  Personal plans:
    · Free        — $0/mo   — 10 invoices
    · Basic       — $9/mo   — 50 invoices
    · Plus        — $19/mo  — 200 invoices
    · Premium     — $39/mo  — Unlimited invoices

  · Upgrade with credit proration on company plans
  · Cron job runs hourly to auto-expire subscriptions past their period end
  · Expired subscriptions block all write operations via middleware
  · Directors notified by in-app notification on expiry
  · Same-plan renewal allowed when subscription is expired
  · Payment modal uses React portal for true full-page backdrop blur

  SUBSCRIPTION LOCK (Layout / SubscriptionLock)
  ───────────────────────────────────────────────
  · When a company subscription expires:
    - Employees and Accountants see a full lock screen:
        "Workspace Access Suspended — contact your Director"
        Only the logout button is available
    - Director sees a red "Subscription Expired — Action Required" banner
        on every page with a "Renew Now" button
    - Director retains access to Settings and Subscription pages
  · Access is automatically restored once the subscription is renewed

  AI ASSISTANT (ai)
  ──────────────────
  · Floating chat panel accessible on all dashboard pages
  · Powered by Llama 3.3 70B via Groq API
  · Injected context: invoice stats, monthly breakdown, top vendors,
    recent invoices, workspace name, current date
  · Answers questions in English and French
  · Number formatting adapted to French locale (spaces as thousands separator)

  ACTIVITY HISTORY (activity)
  ────────────────────────────
  · Every significant action is logged to activity_log
  · Tracked actions:
      invoice.created, invoice.status_changed, invoice.updated,
      invoice.deleted, member.joined, member.left, invitation.rejected,
      company.updated, membership.expired, contract.renewed
  · Filterable history page for audit review
  · JSONB metadata stored per event (user name, old/new values, etc.)

  GLOBAL SEARCH (Topbar)
  ───────────────────────
  · Search bar in the topbar — press Enter to navigate to Invoices
    with the query pre-filled
  · Invoice list debounces search input (400ms) and filters server-side
  · Searches vendor name, invoice number, and created-by name

  DARK MODE (Theme)
  ──────────────────
  · Full light / dark mode via next-themes
  · Toggle button in topbar
  · All components use CSS custom properties (--background, --foreground,
    --primary, --muted, --border, --card) for automatic theme adaptation
  · Dark-mode variants on all status badges, notification panels,
    report dropdowns, chat bubbles, and lock screens


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  DATABASE SCHEMA (18 tables)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  users                 — Accounts (email, password hash, verification codes)
  workspaces            — Personal or company workspaces
  companies             — Company profiles linked 1:1 to a workspace
  roles                 — Predefined roles (Admin, Director, Accountant,
                          Employee, Personal)
  memberships           — User↔Workspace link with role and contract dates
  invoices              — Invoice records (vendor, amount, currency, status)
  invoice_status        — Status enum (draft, pending_review, approved,
                          rejected, paid, archived)
  extracted_fields      — OCR-extracted fields per invoice
  documents             — Files attached to invoices
  status_history        — Audit trail of every invoice status change
  invitations           — Join/leave/renewal requests between users and
                          company workspaces
  subscription_plans    — Plan tiers with price and usage limits
  subscriptions         — Active subscription per user or company
  notifications         — In-app notifications per user with read status
  activity_log          — Workspace activity audit log with JSONB metadata
  chat_conversations    — Channels and direct message threads
  chat_members          — Conversation participants with last_read_at tracking
  chat_messages         — Message content (soft-delete via deleted_at)


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  SECURITY
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  · JWT tokens with 24-hour expiry on every request
  · Email verification required before first login
  · bcryptjs password hashing (10 salt rounds)
  · Every API route behind authenticate() middleware
  · Workspace isolation: every query scoped to workspace_id membership check
  · Role enforcement at route level (authorizeInWorkspace, authorizeAdmin)
  · Parameterized SQL queries throughout (no raw string concatenation)
  · CORS: credentials allowed, origin matched per request
  · Helmet.js for HTTP security headers (XSS, MIME sniffing, etc.)
  · Subscription middleware blocks all write operations when expired


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  BACKGROUND JOBS (CRON)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  checkAndExpireSubscriptions()   — Runs hourly
    Finds company subscriptions past current_period_end, marks them
    'expired', sends a notification to the workspace Director.

  removeExpiredContracts()        — Runs daily at midnight + on startup
    Finds Accountant memberships where contract_end < NOW(), removes them,
    resets their active workspace to personal, sends them a notification.
